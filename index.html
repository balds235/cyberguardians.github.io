About Us

Welcome to CyberGuardians, the cybersecurity club at Larchmont Charter School: Lafayette Campus! Our mission is to educate, empower, and protect the digital world through cybersecurity awareness and skills.

Our Goals

  • To promote cybersecurity literacy among students
  • To provide hands-on learning experiences in cybersecurity
  • To foster a culture of security-consciousness and responsibility

We host various events and activities to keep you engaged and up-to-date with the latest cybersecurity trends. Check our events page for upcoming sessions!
